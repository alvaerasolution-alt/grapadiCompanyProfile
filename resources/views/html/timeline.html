Key moments that shaped our history.

2010

Founded in Jakarta

Ken Research began its operations with a small team of 2 passionate analysts, aiming to bridge the gap in Asian market intelligence.

2015

Establishing our footprint in the Middle East, we opened our first international office to serve the growing demand in the MENA region.
